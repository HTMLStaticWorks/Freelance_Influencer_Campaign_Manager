Our Story

Founded in 2024, InfluencerHub started with a simple mission: to make influencer marketing accessible, transparent, and data-driven for brands of all sizes. We realized that traditional agencies were too slow and manual platforms were too fragmented.

Today, we empower thousands of freelancers and brands to collaborate on campaigns that don't just look good—they perform. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI matching with human-centric support to ensure every partnership is a success.
